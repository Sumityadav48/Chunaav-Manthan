Cold Calling

We provide a call helpline number where the voters can call on the toll free number and can raise any issue about the constituency or can get an update about the candidate.

What Exactly our Political Call Centers do ?

These are principally outbound call centers designed to promote a particular candidate, political action committee (PAC) or party's goals and objectives. The three main tasks are getting out the vote, fundraising, and conducting research.

What we Include




Comprehensive Caller Details

Volunteers need to know exactly who they're speaking to so that they can target their information to the receiver. A call center solution that lacks comprehensive caller information wastes a huge amount of time and resources. Candidates should not lose elections because their call center software has volunteers contacting the wrong individuals.




Call Disposition Codes

After volunteers complete calls, call disposition codes allow them to record how the calls went. Therefore if the recipient screamed into the phone that they hate your candidate, you could avoid calling the number again. An advanced version of this feature is available in CRM Integrations which allow supervisors to see an overview of all calls, their outcomes, and how individual volunteers are performing.




Local Phone Numbers

Most people prefer to receive calls from within their own area code. Unsurprisingly, this is especially true for constituents looking for candidates with a local presence and concern for the area's needs. Call centers can employ multiple local phone numbers or even toll-free numbers for advertising campaigns.




Call Recording

Political call center volunteers serve as representatives for campaigns. As a result, it is imperative that their presentation is of high quality. Call Recording allows coordinators and managers to ensure their volunteers are meeting standards.
